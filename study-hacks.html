Study Hacks That Actually Work

By Harvay & Alfred

1. Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25mins, rest for 5. It keeps your mind sharp and prevent burnout.

2. Active Recall

Close your notes and try to explain what you learned. It strengthens memory.

3. Spaced Repetition

Review lessons gradually over days instead of cramming everything at once.

4. Remove Distractions

Turn off notifications and keep only the things you need on your desk.

5. Teach What You Learned

Explain the topic to someone else it helps you understand it better.
