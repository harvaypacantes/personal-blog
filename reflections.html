What we realize as a first year BSIT student

Being a first year BSIT student has been exciting but challenging for us.

At first, we felt overwhelmed by new concepts and fast-paced lessons.

But we realized that learning IT is not just about memorizing codes,

its about problem solving,patience and persistance. Mistakes became lessons

and small achivements built our confidence. We've learned that asking questions

and collaborating with classmates are essential for growth. This journey taught

us to embrace challenges and enjoy learning step by step.
