The Journey Behind Learners Duo

By Harvay & Alfred

Learners Duo began as a simple idea - two first-year IT students sharing their learning adventures. From coding basics to productivity hacks, every post is written to help other beginners grow.

"We believe learning is easier when shared - that's why Learners Duo exists."

How We Started

As first-year IT students, we struggled with new concepts, deadlines, and complex lessons. So, we began documenting our notes and transforming them into helpful articles.

What We Aim to Share

We focus on publishing study tips, coding tutorials, real student experiences, and guides that make IT easier for everyone starting out.
